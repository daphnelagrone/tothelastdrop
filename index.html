To The Last Drop Coffee Shoppe

To the Last Drop Coffee Shoppe is a small cafe in a little town called Trenton, Tennessee that serves a variety of gourmet sandwiches, soups and desserts. We specialize in lattes, cappuccinos, and have a wide variety of medium to bold roasted coffees. Come down and say hi to our general manager, Lori Michel, and buy some delicious treats!
