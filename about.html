About Us

To the Last Drop is a small coffee shop in Trenton, Tennessee. We opened on February 7, 2008, and have worked to provide good food and a pleasant atmosphere to all of our customers every day!

We specialize in medium to bold coffees, cappuccinos, and lattes. We also serve breakfast and lunch with a wide variety of foods to choose from. This includes soups, salads, sandwiches, and even desserts. Check out our menu page to see all we have to offer!
